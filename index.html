Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing.

Which blockchains are currently supported?

BlockTrace natively supports Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Solana, and Avalanche. We are continuously indexing new layer-2 networks and app-chains.

How accurate is the AI entity resolution?

Our models run continuous heuristics combining on-chain behavior, off-chain data leaks, and known exploit patterns, yielding an industry-leading 99.4% attribution accuracy rate.

Can we integrate the risk oracle into our smart contracts?

Yes. Enterprise clients have access to our on-chain Risk Oracle, allowing smart contracts to programmatically reject interactions from flagged addresses in real-time.

Do you offer custom SLA for high-throughput APIs?

Absolutely. Our Enterprise tier includes custom rate limits, dedicated RPC endpoints, and a 99.99% uptime SLA guarantee backed by 24/7 technical support.
